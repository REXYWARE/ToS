REXYWARE : Terms of Service

Section : 1.- REXYWARE Points

Section : 1.1 Distribution of REXYWARE points.

REXYWARE Points, (a custom made currency system) are a specific currency that can only be granted by HR's (higher ranks), for instance, the Owner of REXYWARE or the Co-Owner. Under any circumstance, a normal staff member is NOT allowed to grant themselves any REXYWARE Points. If you are caught doing so you will be immediately sanctioned for your actions under section 3, all dependent on how much damage you caused.

Section : 2.- Giveaways

Section : 2.1 Giveaway holders.

Giveaways are only to be started by staff members who have the @Administrator or @Moderator role. If someone without those ranks starts a giveaway and they are caught, they will be granted a sanction under section 3.

Section : 2.2 Unfair/Biased giveaways.

If you are eligible to post a giveaway, but you have biased it by making 2 choices or more have the same answer, you will be asked to remake the poll to an equal bias. Failure to comply or to co-operate will result in a sanction under section 3.

Section : 3.- Sanctions

Section : 3.1 Who is allowed to grant sanctions?

Only the higher ranks (Owner and Co-Owner) and members of staff with the @Moderator or @Administrator roles are allowed to grant sanctions to members of the community. Only higher ranks (Owner and Co-Owner) are allowed to sanction other staff members. If you would like to report a staff member, please send a direct message to a high rank (Owner and Co-Owner) and state issues and if you can, send screenshots for further proof.

Section : 3.2 What are the current sanctions?

The current sanctions in place are, removal of REXYWARE Points, revoked REXYWARE Premium subscription, blacklisting, fired from staff team, banned from REXYWARE server(s), and last but not least, banned from all of REXYWARE's games and services.

Section : 4.- Offsite controversy.

Section : 4.1 Reporting and discovery.

If you have had some controversy online for something that was said on an account that YOU owned, if a member of staff reports or finds any 'solidified' proof of the incident, the member WILL gain a sanction under section 3. If a community member without any special roles sends a report of some offsite controversy, tell them (if they aren't already) to speak to a moderator, or to create a ticket and the member, if found in server, will be sanctioned, again under section 3.

Section : 5.- Omitted media.

Section : 5.1 Offensive comments

If you are seen or caught posting offensive comments or images within the server or under section 4, you WILL be sanctioned under section 3, depending on severity.

Section : 6.- Notifying guild members.

Section : 6.1 Notifying everyone in the guild.

If you have notified everyone in the guild/server in an accidental cause, please message a representative or state a VERY good reason for notifying everyone, if it was for a casual ping, we have roles for that, if this was a repeated action, you will be sanctioned under section 3.
